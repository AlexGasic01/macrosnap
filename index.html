Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → find MacroSnap → tap Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open MacroSnap, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore" in the top right corner. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

The AI scan results look wrong — what do I do?

Tap "Fix Results" on the scan results screen to manually adjust calories and macros. For best results, make sure your food is well-lit and clearly visible in the photo.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to the Profile tab in the app → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". This will permanently delete all your data. You can also email us at alexgasic@yahoo.com to request deletion.

I was charged but the app says I'm not subscribed

Tap "Restore" on the paywall screen to restore your purchase. If the issue persists, email us at alexgasic@yahoo.com with your Apple ID and we'll resolve it promptly.

What does MacroSnap do with my food photos?

Food photos are sent to the Anthropic Claude API for AI-powered nutrition analysis. Photos are not stored on our servers beyond what is needed to process your request. See our Privacy Policy below for full details.
