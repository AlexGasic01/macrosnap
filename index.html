Welcome to MacroSnap ("the App"), an AI-powered calorie and nutrition tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By using MacroSnap, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

MacroSnap is operated by Alex Gasic, based in Australia. If you have any questions, contact us at alexgasic@yahoo.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use MacroSnap:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers.
  • Health and nutrition data: Height, weight, goal weight, age, gender, dietary preferences, and daily calorie and macro targets you provide during onboarding.
  • Food log data: Meals, photos of food, and nutrition information you log within the App.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and screens visited.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system, used for technical support and app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalise the MacroSnap experience, including calculating your nutrition targets and tracking your progress.

  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
